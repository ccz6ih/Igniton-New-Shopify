# Brand

## Positioning

Igniton is a quantum wellness supplement brand. The product line centers on **IgniCognition** (cognitive performance) and **IgniLongevity** (healthspan). The storefront should read as premium, science-forward, and calm — not clinical, not "wellness-influencer."

## What to carry forward from the old site

- Core brand story and product claims
- SKU structure, bundle pricing logic, and subscription offer mechanics
- Trust signals that converted: science citations, founder voice, before/after framing

## What to leave behind

- The 12-scheme color system — too many variants diluted the identity.
- Heavy gradient buttons and oversized shadows — dated.
- Dawn-override CSS sprawl — replace with a small, deliberate token set (see [design-system.md](design-system.md)).

## Voice

- Confident, specific, numeric where possible.
- No hedging ("may help support"). Use approved claim language from legal.
- Short sentences. Active voice. Second person on CTAs.

## Prior reference (for continuity only, not to copy)

Old palette: `#1B1B57` dark blue, `#191880` bright blue, `#D3A23C` gold. Old type: Poppins headings, Quicksand body. Use only as a starting point to react against or refine.
